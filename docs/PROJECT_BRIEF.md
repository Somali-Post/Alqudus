# Alqudus Express Trucking LLC — Project Brief

## Project name

Alqudus Express Trucking LLC website and future driver operations platform.

## Business model

Alqudus Express Trucking LLC works with owner-operators. The company does not provide trucks to drivers. Drivers must have their own truck and equipment. Alqudus Express helps connect qualified drivers with contract/job opportunities and earns a commission from completed work.

## Main website purpose

The website should focus on recruiting qualified owner-operators and collecting driver applications online.

The website should clearly explain:

- Alqudus Express is based in Saint Cloud, MN
- The company works with drivers who already own their own truck
- Drivers can apply to join the company’s driver network
- The company helps connect approved drivers with contract/job opportunities
- The company can later manage driver records, documents, jobs, and commission tracking through a system

## Primary audience

Owner-operators who:

- Own their own truck
- Are looking for contract/load opportunities
- Want dispatch or job support
- Are willing to work through a commission-based arrangement

## Secondary audience

- Brokers
- Shippers/customers
- Business partners
- Insurance/compliance contacts
- General visitors who want to verify the company

## Core value proposition

**Bring your truck. We help bring the work.**

## Phase 1 goal

Build a low-cost prototype website that helps the company look professional and begin collecting driver applications.

## Phase 1 features

- Homepage
- Owner-operator recruitment section
- Driver requirements section
- Driver application form
- Contact section
- Mobile-friendly design
- Logo and brand colors applied
- Application submissions sent to email or stored in a simple backend/Google Sheet depending on budget

## Future platform vision

The website can later become a full driver operations platform with:

- Admin dashboard
- Driver application management
- Driver document uploads
- Document expiry tracking
- Job/load assignment
- Commission tracking
- Driver payout records
- Proof of delivery upload
- WhatsApp/SMS notifications
- Driver portal

## Design direction

The design should be clean, professional, blue/navy, and driver-focused. It should use the approved Alqudus Express logo as the visual guide.

## Important business wording

Make it clear that drivers need their own truck.

Preferred wording:

- Owner-operators wanted
- Drivers with their own truck
- Contract opportunities
- Join our driver network
- Apply to work with Alqudus Express

Avoid wording:

- We provide trucks
- Get a truck from us
- Guaranteed loads
- Employee driver positions, unless confirmed by the client
