# Alqudus Express Trucking LLC — MVP Plan

## MVP objective

Build a simple, low-cost website that allows Alqudus Express Trucking LLC to recruit owner-operators and receive applications online.

## MVP scope

The MVP should avoid complex features and focus on the highest-value first step: driver recruitment.

## MVP pages

### 1. Home

Purpose: introduce the company and explain the business model.

Suggested hero message:

**Owner-operators wanted**

Suggested subheading:

**Bring your truck. We help connect you with contract opportunities.**

Primary call-to-action:

**Apply Now**

Secondary call-to-action:

**Contact Us**

### 2. For Owner-Operators

Purpose: explain who the company is looking for.

Content should cover:

- Drivers must have their own truck
- Alqudus Express helps connect drivers with contract/job opportunities
- Commission-based business model
- Basic requirements
- How the process works

### 3. Apply

Purpose: collect driver applications.

Initial application fields:

- Full name
- Phone number
- Email
- City/state
- Do you own your own truck?
- Truck type
- Trailer type
- CDL status
- Insurance status
- Years of experience
- Preferred routes/lanes
- Availability
- Notes/message

### 4. Contact

Purpose: make it easy to reach the company.

Include:

- Phone
- Email
- WhatsApp, if used
- Saint Cloud, MN
- Business hours, if available

## MVP technical options

### Cheapest version

- Static website
- Contact/application form connected to email or Google Forms
- No login
- No database
- No admin dashboard

Best for very low budget.

### Better prototype

- Website with database-backed application form
- Simple admin view for applications
- Ability to update application status
- Basic document upload later

Best if the client wants to manage applications professionally from the start.

## MVP success criteria

The MVP is successful if:

- The company can share the website link with drivers
- Drivers understand that they need their own truck
- Drivers can apply online
- The company receives applications in an organized way
- The website looks professional and trustworthy

## Suggested phased pricing language

Phase 1 should be positioned as the affordable first step.

Suggested wording:

> We can start with a simple professional website and driver application form first. This keeps the cost low and allows the company to start recruiting drivers quickly. Later, once applications and jobs increase, we can upgrade it into a full operations system.

## Future phases

### Phase 2 — Admin dashboard and driver records

- View applications
- Approve/reject drivers
- Store driver profiles
- Track document status

### Phase 3 — Job and commission system

- Create jobs/loads
- Assign jobs to drivers
- Track job status
- Calculate company commission
- Track driver payout

### Phase 4 — Driver portal and notifications

- Driver login
- View assigned jobs
- Upload proof of delivery
- Upload documents
- Update availability
- Receive SMS/WhatsApp/email notifications
