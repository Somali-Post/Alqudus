## How it works

### Step 1 — Apply online

Submit your details, truck information, experience, and availability.

### Step 2 — Get reviewed

Our team reviews your application and confirms your equipment, documents, and readiness.

### Step 3 — Join the network

Approved drivers can be considered for contract/job opportunities through Alqudus Express.

### Step 4 — Complete jobs

Drivers complete assigned work using their own truck while Alqudus Express manages coordination and commission records.
